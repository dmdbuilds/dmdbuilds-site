The story

Who I am

I started with FIRST Lego League and never stopped building. FTC software lead and main driver on Team 23304, now competing on FRC Team 7419 where I led impact video production for two regionals.

I build real infrastructure. Jarvis runs on GCP, Cloud Run, and PostgreSQL. Gesture control runs live on my MacBook using MediaPipe. These aren't school projects, they're products.

I also care deeply about Hinduism and Sanskrit, am part of TKS, and I'm documenting everything publicly at dmdbuilds.dev. The chaos is the content.
